            <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold text-center mb-8 text-slate-800">Pod Structure & Roles</h2>

            <div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-4 gap-6">

                <div class="role-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg text-center">

                    <h3 class="font-bold text-xl text-[#0369EA] mb-2">Pod Leader</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-700">Acts as the lead for the pod, facilitates communication, and delegates tasks during unplanned absences.</p>

                </div>

                <div class="role-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg text-center">

                    <h3 class="font-bold text-xl text-[#0369EA] mb-2">Primary Owner</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-700">Has day-to-day responsibility for their assigned accounts and maintains the "Living Document".</p>

                </div>

                <div class="role-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg text-center">

                    <h3 class="font-bold text-xl text-[#0369EA] mb-2">Primary Backup</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-700">Has deep familiarity with the accounts and serves as the main point of contact during an absence.</p>

                </div>

                <div class="role-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg text-center">

                    <h3 class="font-bold text-xl text-[#0369EA] mb-2">The Rest of the Pod</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-700">Serve as general support for all accounts within the pod and can be consulted as needed.</p>

                </div>

            </div>

        </section>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm">Access to 'Get Briefed' and other relevant call recordings.</p>

                </div>

                 <div class="p-4 rounded-lg bg-slate-50 border border-slate-200">

                    <h3 class="font-semibold text-lg text-[#0369EA]">Named Contacts</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm">Key customer contacts clearly listed in Gainsight and SFDC.</p>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm">Documented in the Action Plan field of the account's success plan.</p>

                 <div class="p-4 rounded-lg bg-slate-50 border border-slate-200">

                </div>

        <section id="handoff" class="mb-12">

             <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold text-center mb-8 text-slate-800">Activating a Backup: The Hand-Off Process</h2>

             <div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-8">

                <div class="flow-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg">

                    <h3 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-4 text-[#0369EA]">For Planned Absences (PTO)</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 mb-4">A proactive hand-off ensures a smooth transition before scheduled time off.</p>

                    <ol class="list-decimal list-inside space-y-3 text-slate-700">

                        <li><span class="font-semibold">Inform the Pod:</span> Post OOO dates and links to relevant "Living Documents" or Success Plans in the Pod's Slack channel.</li>

                        <li><span class="font-semibold">Update Systems:</span> Set your OOO on Gmail to direct inquiries to the Pod Google Group, and on Slack to name the specific Primary Backup for key accounts.</li>

                        <li><span class="font-semibold">Brief if Needed (Optional):</span> Schedule a quick call with the Pod if complex issues require a verbal hand-off.</li>

                    </ol>

                </div>

                <div class="flow-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg">

                    <h3 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-4 text-[#0369EA]">For Unplanned Absences</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 mb-4">A clear protocol for when team members are unexpectedly out of office.</p>

                     <ol class="list-decimal list-inside space-y-3 text-slate-700">

                        <li><span class="font-semibold">Manager Communicates:</span> The manager informs the Pod Leader that a team member is unexpectedly OOO.</li>

                        <li><span class="font-semibold">Pod Leader Delegates:</span> The Pod Leader reviews the absent TAM's calendar and delegates urgent tasks (ticket reviews, customer calls, etc.) to the Primary Backup.</li>

                        <li><span class="font-semibold">Backup Communicates:</span> The Primary Backup informs the account's named contacts about the temporary absence to manage expectations.</li>

                    </ol>

                </div>

             </div>

        </section>


        <section id="tools" class="mb-12">

            <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold text-center mb-8 text-slate-800">Tools, Documents & When to Use Them</h2>

            <div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3 gap-6">

                <div class="tool-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg">

                    <h3 class="font-bold text-xl mb-2">"Living Document"</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm"><span class="font-semibold text-[#0369EA]">What:</span> A centralized document (Google Doc, Confluence page) with all key account info.</p>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm mt-2"><span class="font-semibold text-[#0369EA]">When:</span> Maintained continuously. It's the first place a backup should look for information.</p>

                </div>

                <div class="tool-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg">

                    <h3 class="font-bold text-xl mb-2">Gong</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm"><span class="font-semibold text-[#0369EA]">What:</span> Call recording and intelligence platform.</p>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm mt-2"><span class="font-semibold text-[#0369EA]">When:</span> Used by backups to get up to speed on recent customer conversations via 'Get Briefed'.</p>

                </div>

                <div class="tool-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg">

                    <h3 class="font-bold text-xl mb-2">Gainsight/SFDC</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm"><span class="font-semibold text-[#0369EA]">What:</span> Customer success and CRM platforms.</p>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm mt-2"><span class="font-semibold text-[#0369EA]">When:</span> Used to find Named Contacts and review Success Plans, especially the Action Plan for current issues.</p>

                </div>

                <div class="tool-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg">

                    <h3 class="font-bold text-xl mb-2">Pod Slack Channel</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm"><span class="font-semibold text-[#0369EA]">What:</span> A private channel for internal pod communication.</p>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm mt-2"><span class="font-semibold text-[#0369EA]">When:</span> Used for daily updates, quick questions, and for the Primary TAM to announce a planned absence.</p>

                </div>

                 <div class="tool-card p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg">

                    <h3 class="font-bold text-xl mb-2">Pod Google Group</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm"><span class="font-semibold text-[#0369EA]">What:</span> An external-facing email alias for the pod.</p>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-sm mt-2"><span class="font-semibold text-[#0369EA]">When:</span> Used in the Primary TAM's Gmail OOO message to catch external inquiries during an absence.</p>

                </div>
